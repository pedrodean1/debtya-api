Last updated: April 9, 2026

You can cancel your DebtYa subscription at any time.

You can cancel:

  • from the billing portal available in your account, or
  • by contacting support at support@debtya.com.

When cancellation takes effect

If you cancel, your subscription remains active through the end of the period already paid, unless expressly stated otherwise.

No new charges will be made once cancellation is effective for the next cycle.

What happens next

  • some service features may become unavailable.
