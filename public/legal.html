Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

You can cancel your DebtYa subscription at any time.

Recurring subscription charges are processed by Stripe. Customer portal links, receipts, and payment-method management may be presented through Stripe interfaces when enabled for your account.

How to cancel

You can cancel:

  • from the billing portal available in your account, or
  • by contacting support at support@debtya.com.

When cancellation takes effect

If you cancel, your subscription remains active through the end of the period already paid, unless expressly stated otherwise.

No new charges will be made once cancellation is effective for the next cycle.

What happens next

After you cancel:

  • you keep access until the end of the paid period,
  • after that period ends, your subscription will not renew,
  • some service features may become unavailable.

Last updated: April 22, 2026

When you cancel, cancellation prevents future renewals but does not automatically refund or prorate the period already billed.

DebtYa may evaluate exceptions case by case, for example:

Politica de Reembolsos

Ultima actualizacion: 22 de abril de 2026

Salvo que la ley aplicable exija lo contrario, los pagos de suscripcion de DebtYa no son reembolsables una vez procesados.

Los cargos se procesan con Stripe como infraestructura de pagos; como aparecen en tu estado de cuenta sigue las convenciones de Stripe y de tu emisor.
